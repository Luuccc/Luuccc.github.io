.privacy-policy

We all just want to enjoy our programs, knowing that we aren't being watched.

Personal data collected

Data collected automatically

- Your IP address and mobile device identifiers (such as your device ID, advertising ID, MAC address and IMEI)
- Data about your device, such as device name and operating system, browser type and language
- Coarse location data (country or city-specific)

Data collected from partners

- Demographic data (such as to determine the coarse location of your IP address)
- Data from platforms that the games run on (such as to verify payment)
- Data for advertising and analytics purposes, so I can keep improving my games

Why data is collected

Service functionality

- To verify and confirm payments correctly
- To provide and deliver products and services that you request
- To allow the services to operate correctly

Fair service

- Analyze and monitor the use of services, to moderate misbehaving players

Personalized advertisements

- Deliver, target and improve our advertising and services, with your consent

Anonymized data

- For all purposes, all data that I may analyze, profile and segment is in anonymized form
- Should your game crash a report will be sent to Unity who will then process and anonymize this data

Sharing of data

As this data is being collected by a third party being Unity, they have access to this composite data as well. We will not share your data with any third party unless required to do so by law or regulation (which is very unlikely).
