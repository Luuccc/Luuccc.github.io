Data Collection
Should your game crash a report will be sent to Unity who will then process and anonymize this data. I can then see within the code where the issue occurred, and on what type of device this happened. User-identifiable data is not kept. You will note that this game does not require special permissions or further access to any personal data of any kind.


What happens to my data?
Your data is stored by Unity and consumed by me in a composite form.


Sharing of data
As this data is being collected by a third party being Unity, they have access to this composite data as well. We will not share your data with any third party unless required to do so by law or regulation (which is very unlikely).
